Step 1

Whisk together the evaporated milk, vanilla, condensed milk, and heavy cream.

Step 2

Poke holes evenly across the cake using the back end of a chopstick.

Step 3

Pour the prepared milk mixture over the cooled cake.

Step 4

Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.

Step 5

If there is excess liquid in the pan, pour it out. The cake should be completely and evenly saturated in the milk mixture.
