Step 1

Start by mixing the eggs one by one. Beat them until they take on an airy texture and become pale in color.

Step 2

Slowly add in the sugar until all of it is incorporated. Then, add in the melted butter and vanilla.

Step 3

Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl. Slowly fold it into the wet ingredients. Do not overmix!

Step 4

Grease and flour a 9x13” pan. Pour the finished batter into the prepped pan. Use the spatula to spread the batter evenly.

Step 5

Bake the cake at 350°F until it is golden brown. Insert a toothpick into the center; if it comes out clean, the cake is done!
