Step 1

Mix together the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla. Use a mixer or whisk to whip the cream until it is thick and fluffy.

Step 2

Slice fresh strawberries into thin slices.

Step 3

Prepare your workspace. Use a piping bag and tip to create an intricate decoration with your whipped cream. Make sure everything is chilled.

Step 4

Use the strawberries generously across the cake, as the acidity of the fruit is important to the flavor. Here, you can add optional additional fruit, cinnamon, or even a drizzle of caramel.

Step 5

Slice, serve, and enjoy your fresh tres leches cake!
